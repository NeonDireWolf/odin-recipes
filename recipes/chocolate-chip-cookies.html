Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

These chocolate chip cookies are the pinnacle of perfection! If you want the kind of cookie you see at bakeries then these are for you.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 3/4 unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 325 degrees F. Grease cookie sheets or line with parchment paper.
  2. Sift flour, baking soda, and salt together.
  3. Beat brown sugar, melted butter, and white sugar with a mixer in a large bowl until smooth.
  4. Beat in egg, egg yolk, and vanilla until light and creamy; add flour mixture and stir until dough is just combined
  5. Stir in chocolate chips.
  6. Drop spoonfuls of dough 3 inches apart onto the prepared baking sheets.
  7. Bake cookies in the preheated oven until edges are golden, about 15 to 17 minutes.
  8. Cool on the cookie sheets briefly before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.
