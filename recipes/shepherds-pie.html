Shepherd's Pie

Description

Tasty ground beef topped with cheesy mashed potatoes. It hits all the spots if you are looking for a hearty comfort food.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs lean ground beef
  • 1 cup chopped onion
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (12-ounce) package frozen mixed veggies
  • 1 1/2 cups low sodium beef broth
  • 1/4 cups tomato paste
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tablespoon quick cooking tapioca, finely crushed
  • 1 teaspoon dried Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 (24 ounce) packages refrigerated mashed potatoes
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • paprika to taste
  • chopped fresh parsley

Steps

  1. Gather all the ingredients
  2. Cook ground beef and onion in a 12 inch skillet over medium heat until beef is browned and onion is tender; around 8 minutes. Add garlic. Cook and stir until fragrant, about 1-2 minutes; drain fat.
  3. Add beef mixture and frozen mixed to a 5 to 6 quart slow cooker.
  4. Whisk together beef broth, tomato paste, Worcestershire sauce, tapioca, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper in a small bowl. Pour over beef mixture; stir to combine.
  5. Cover and cook on low for 4 to 6 hours or High for 3 hours.
  6. Prepare mashed potatoes according to the package directions. Stir in cheese. Spread atop the beef mixture.
  7. Cover and cook on High for 20 minutes.
  8. Garnish with paprika and parsley.
